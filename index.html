Featured Work

As part of my Strategic Communication coursework, I designed a promotional poster highlighting Adobe MAX, the annual creativity conference, tailored for students in the STCO program. The project emphasized both visual design and strategic messaging, requiring me to apply Adobe Creative Cloud tools while aligning with professional design principles. I incorporated strong typography, balanced layouts, and an engaging color scheme to capture attention and communicate the relevance of the event to creative professionals. This project not only strengthened my technical design skills in Adobe software but also demonstrated my ability to translate brand identity and complex information into a clear, visually compelling piece.
